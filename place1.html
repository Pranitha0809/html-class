Thailand Travel Packages

Often includes basic accommodation, pre-booked intercity transport (bus/train), and key activities, giving more freedom.
Package Type Typical Duration & Destinations Key Inclusions & Focus
Classic Intro 5-7 Nights (Bangkok & Pattaya OR Bangkok & Phuket) Includes major city sights, an island excursion (e.g., Coral Island), transfers, and accommodation.
North & South 10-14 Nights (Bangkok, Chiang Mai, & an Island: Phuket/Krabi/Samui) A comprehensive trip combining culture, mountains, temples, and beach relaxation. Often includes domestic flights.
Honeymoon/Luxury 7-11 Nights (Phuket, Krabi, or Koh Samui) Focuses on luxury resorts, private transfers, sunset cruises, couples' spa treatments, and private excursions.

  • 7 Days (Focus on North & Central)

  • 10 Days (Focus on Central & South):
    1. Day 1-3: Bangkok (City & Day trip to Ayutthaya).

  • 14 Days (The Full Experience):
